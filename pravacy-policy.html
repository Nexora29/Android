Privacy Policy for SnapScanner

Effective Date: May 12, 2026

Thank you for using SnapScanner.

SnapScanner respects your privacy and is designed with an offline-first approach to help keep your documents secure on your device.

This Privacy Policy explains how SnapScanner handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

SnapScanner does not collect, sell, rent, or share your personal documents with third parties.

The app primarily operates offline on your device.

Depending on the features you use, SnapScanner may access:

  • Camera access (to scan documents)
  • Device storage access (to save and manage files)
  • Optional microphone or media access if enabled by future features

Your scanned documents, OCR text, and exported files remain stored locally on your device unless you choose to export or share them yourself.

2. OCR and AI Processing

SnapScanner performs OCR (Optical Character Recognition) and AI-based document enhancement locally on your device whenever possible.

Your scanned files are not uploaded to external servers for processing.

3. Data Sharing

SnapScanner does not share your personal data or scanned documents with third parties.

We do not sell user data.

We do not use third-party advertising networks that track users across applications.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to help protect your information and scanned documents.

However, no method of electronic storage is completely secure. Users are responsible for securing access to their own devices.

5. Permissions

SnapScanner may request the following permissions:

Camera Permission

Used to capture and scan documents.

Storage Permission

Used to save, organize, import, and export scanned files.

Permissions are only used for core app functionality.

6. Offline Usage

SnapScanner is designed to support offline usage for scanning, OCR, and document management features.

Some future cloud-based features may require internet access. If introduced, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

7. Children’s Privacy

SnapScanner is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Any updates will be reflected within the application or official distribution platforms.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact the SnapScanner support team through the official application page.

Your Privacy Matters

SnapScanner was built with a focus on privacy, speed, and offline productivity.

Your documents stay under your control.
